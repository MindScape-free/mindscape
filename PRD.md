# MindScape: Product Requirements Document

## 1. What is MindScape?
MindScape is a digital tool designed to help people brainstorm, study, and organize their thoughts using visual maps. Instead of just writing lists of notes, MindScape creates "mind maps"—visual webs of information where central ideas branch out into smaller, related details. It uses artificial intelligence (AI) to help build these maps automatically based on whatever you are thinking about or the documents you provide.

## 2. Why does MindScape exist? 
When we learn something new or plan a big project, our brains often feel overwhelmed by too much information. MindScape exists to clear that mental fog. It helps by:
- Turning long, boring text into easy-to-read visual layouts.
- Making it easy to "zoom in" on small details without losing sight of the big picture.
- Providing a smart assistant to answer questions in the moment.

## 3. Who is it for?
MindScape is built for anyone who needs to understand or organize information better:
- **Students** who want to break down complex subjects for exams.
- **Teachers** who want to create visual aids for their classes.
- **Writers and Creators** who need to map out storylines or project plans.
- **Lifelong Learners** who enjoy exploring new topics in a structured way.

## 4. Key Features and How They Work

### 4.1. Instant Visual Map Generation
You can give MindScape a topic, an image, or a document (like a PDF), and it will automatically generate a visual map. 
- **Side-by-Side Comparison:** You can ask it to compare two different things, and it will build a map showing how they are similar and how they are different.
- **Deep Explanations:** You can choose how much detail you want. Whether you want a quick summary or a very deep dive into a subject, the tool adjusts its work to match your needs.

### 4.2. "Infinite" Maps (Going Deeper)
If you find a specific part of your map interesting, you can click on it to create a *new* map just for that sub-topic. This allows you to explore ideas as deeply as you want, moving from one map into another.

### 4.3. MindSpark: Your AI Study Assistant
Inside the tool, there is a chat window where a smart assistant named MindSpark lives. 
- **contextual Knowledge:** MindSpark knows exactly what is on your current map. You can ask it to explain a specific part or give you more examples.
- **File Reading:** You can "hand" it pictures or documents, and it will read them for you and help add that information to your map.
- **Exporting:** If you have a great conversation with MindSpark, you can save the whole chat as a document to read later.

### 4.4. Active Learning with Quizzes
MindScape doesn't just show you information; it helps you remember it.
- **Custom Quizzes:** The tool can generate quizzes based on your maps. 
- **Smart Learning:** It tracks which questions you get right and which ones you get wrong. It then focuses future quizzes on your "weak spots" to help you master the material faster.

### 4.5. Progress Tracking and Awards
To keep you motivated, MindScape tracks how much time you spend studying and how many maps you've created.
- **Streaks:** It recognizes when you show up several days in a row.
- **Badges:** You can earn virtual awards (like bronze or gold badges) for reaching different milestones.

## 5. Behind the Scenes (In Plain Terms)
While MindScape does a lot of complex work under the hood, the experience for the user is simple and safe:
- **Secure Logins:** When you sign up, your account is protected so that only you can access your private maps.
- **Automatic Saving:** As you work, your maps are saved in the background so you never have to worry about losing your progress.
- **Reliability:** The system is designed to be smart and resilient. If one of the "brain centers" (the AI) is busy or slow, it can automatically try another way to get your answer without you ever knowing there was a hiccup.
- **Real-Time Workspace:** Everything happens instantly. When you make a change, it is remembered immediately across all your devices.
